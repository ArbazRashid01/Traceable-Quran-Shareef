بِسْمِ ٱللَّهِ ٱلرَّحْمَـٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ
١“Alif, Lam, Meem. [Letters whose meaning is known only to Allah.]”
٢“This is the Book in which there is no doubt — a guidance for those mindful of Allah.”
٣“Who believe in the unseen, establish prayer, and spend out of what We have provided for them.”
٤“And who believe in what was revealed to you and what was revealed before you, and of the Hereafter they are certain.”
٥“Those are upon guidance from their Lord, and it is those who are the successful.”
٦“Indeed, those who disbelieve — it is all the same whether you warn them or not — they will not believe.”
٧“Allah has set a seal upon their hearts and upon their hearing, and over their vision is a veil. For them is a great punishment.”
٨“And among the people are those who say, "We believe in Allah and the Last Day," but they are not believers.”
٩“They seek to deceive Allah and those who believe, but they deceive none except themselves, and they perceive it not.”

١٠“In their hearts is a disease, and Allah has increased their disease. For them is a painful punishment because they used to lie.”
١١“And when it is said to them, "Do not cause corruption on the earth," they say, "We are only reformers."”
١٢“Unquestionably, it is they who are the corrupters, but they perceive it not.”
١٣“And when it is said to them, "Believe as the people have believed," they say, "Should we believe as the fools have believed?" — Beware, it is they who are the fools, but they do not know.”
١٤“And when they meet those who believe, they say, "We believe"; but when they are alone with their evil ones, they say, "Indeed, we are with you; we were only mockers."”
١٥“[But] Allāh mocks them and prolongs them in their transgression [while] they wander blindly.”
١٦“Those are the ones who have purchased error [in exchange] for guidance, so their transaction has brought no profit, nor were they guided.”
١٧“Their example is that of one who kindled a fire, but when it illuminated what was around him, Allāh took away their light and left them in darkness [so] they could not see.”

١٨“Deaf, dumb and blind - so they will not return [to the right path].”
١٩“Or [it is] like a rainstorm from the sky within which is darkness, thunder and lightning. They put their fingers in their ears against the thunderclaps in dread of death. But Allāh is encompassing1 of the disbelievers.”
٢٠“The lightning almost snatches away their sight. Every time it lights [the way] for them, they walk therein; but when darkness comes over them, they stand [still]. And if Allāh had willed, He could have taken away their hearing and their sight. Indeed, Allāh is over all things competent.”
٢١“O mankind, worship your Lord, who created you and those before you, that you may become righteous -”
٢٢“[He] who made for you the earth a bed [spread out] and the sky a ceiling and sent down from the sky, rain and brought forth thereby fruits as provision for you. So do not attribute to Allāh equals while you know [that there is nothing similar to Him].”
٢٣“And if you are in doubt about what We have sent down [i.e., the Qur’ān] upon Our Servant [i.e., Prophet Muḥammad (ﷺ)], then produce a sūrah the like thereof and call upon your witnesses [i.e., supporters] other than Allāh, if you should be truthful.”

٢٤“But if you do not - and you will never be able to - then fear the Fire, whose fuel is people and stones, prepared for the disbelievers.”
٢٥“And give good tidings to those who believe and do righteous deeds that they will have gardens [in Paradise] beneath which rivers flow. Whenever they are provided with a provision of fruit therefrom, they will say, "This is what we were provided with before." And it is given to them in likeness. And they will have therein purified spouses, and they will abide therein eternally.”
٢٦“Indeed, Allāh is not timid to present an example - that of a mosquito or what is smaller1 than it. And those who have believed know that it is the truth from their Lord. But as for those who disbelieve, they say, "What did Allāh intend by this as an example?" He misleads many thereby and guides many thereby. And He misleads not except the defiantly disobedient,”
٢٧“Who break the covenant of Allāh after contracting it and sever that which Allāh has ordered to be joined and cause corruption on earth. It is those who are the losers.”
٢٨“How can you disbelieve in Allāh when you were lifeless and He brought you to life; then He will cause you to die, then He will bring you [back] to life, and then to Him you will be returned.”
